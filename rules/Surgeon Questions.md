## Here are some important questions to ask your surgeon before surgery:
Why do I need this surgery? Understand the necessity of the procedure. 
What are the risks and benefits? Inquire about potential complications and advantages. 
What are the alternatives? Ask if there are other treatment options available. 
How will the surgery be performed? Get details about the procedure and techniques used. Will the procedure be Open, Laproscopic, or Robot Assisted?
What type of anesthesia will be used? Understand how you will be kept comfortable during the operation.
